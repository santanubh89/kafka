==========Starting Zookeeper==========
.\zookeeper-server-start.bat ..\..\config\zookeeper.properties

==========Starting Kafka==========
.\kafka-server-start.bat ..\..\config\server.properties

==========Create a Topic==========
.\kafka-topics.bat --bootstrap-server localhost:9092 --topic order_topic --create --partitions 3 --replication-factor 1

==========List all Topics==========
.\kafka-topics.bat --bootstrap-server localhost:9092 --list

==========Describe a Topic==========
.\kafka-topics.bat --bootstrap-server localhost:9092 --topic order_topic --describe

==========Delete a Topic==========
.\kafka-topics.bat --bootstrap-server localhost:9092 --topic order_topic --delete

==========Kafka CLI Producer==========
.\kafka-console-producer.bat --bootstrap-server localhost:9092 --topic order_topic
.\kafka-console-producer.bat --bootstrap-server localhost:9092 --topic order_topic --producer-property acks=all

==========Kafka CLI Consumer==========
.\kafka-console-consumer.bat  --bootstrap-server localhost:9092 --topic order_topic
.\kafka-console-consumer.bat  --bootstrap-server localhost:9092 --topic order_topic --from-beginning
.\kafka-console-consumer.bat  --bootstrap-server localhost:9092 --topic order_topic --group order-processors
