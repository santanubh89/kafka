Create a Topic:
kafka-topics.bat --bootstrap-server localhost:9092 --create --topic application_log --partitions 3 --replication-factor 1

List topics:
kafka-topics.bat --bootstrap-server localhost:9092 --list

Describe a Topic:
kafka-topics.bat --bootstrap-server localhost:9092 --describe --topic application_log

Update number of partitions:
kafka-topics.bat --bootstrap-server localhost:9092 --alter --topic application_log --partitions 3

Delete a Topic:
kafka-topics.bat --bootstrap-server localhost:9092 --delete --topic application_log
